About Us

A quest for sustainable and healthy living. That is what got us looking for eco-friendly products in India. And we decided to build Ecosters.com. Ecosters.com promotes all environment friendly products, products that enable the earth to be more green and products that enable a sustainable living. In the beta version, users would come to Ecosters to

View a consolidated list of eco friendly products in a single site and compare prices with ease
Connect with like minded people and get new ideas for green living
Read the latest articles related to sustainable living

In future Ecosters.com strives to become a niche online supermarket for eco friendly and healthier products and be known for quality, trustworthiness, ease of shopping and environment responsibility. We believe that all people, given the choice between a regular product and eco-friendly product, would choose the latter. Our goal is to provide that eco-friendly or healthier alternative to our customers with the best of shopping experience and thereby help make earth a tinge greener than what we inherited.
